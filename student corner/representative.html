Representative / CR

A class Representative is usually the leader of a student body class, and presides over its class cabinet or organization within a student council who represents the views of his or her class on academic matters to the lecturer, Department and Faculty.

Class representatives chosen

Being elected as a class representative means being chosen alongside another representative, by means of a vote, to represent the whole class. To fully execute his/her duties, the representative confers with the whole class as often as possible and at least before each Class Council meeting.

Role of Class Representative

Class Representatives ensure that students' views on academic matters are heard. They help ensure the University continually listens to and engages with students to improve teaching, learning, assessment and academic services.

Class Representative Qualities

The perfect candidate Someone who is willing to take on the task, Aware, Organised, Diplomatic and sensitive, A good communicator, Good at encouraging people to volunteer or participate, Up to speed on email.
