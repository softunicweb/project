Assigment

An assigment is a task or piece of work that you are given to do, aspecially as part of your studies.
OR
You can refer to someone beging given a particular task or job as their assignment to the taskor job.

Tips of Assignment

Teacher will give you an assignment and you will have to submit it on or before the deadline

  • Before starting you assignment study your book and lecture notes you have taken from you teacher
  • You can also search your topic on internet and gather as much information as you can
  • Analyze the gathered information and make a rough sketch of the assignment
  • Start working on your assignment and complete it with the given time
  • Submit the assignment to the CR on the deadline
